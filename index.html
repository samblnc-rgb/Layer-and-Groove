From custom dividers to storage essentials, we build simple and functional accessories to help you organize and display your records.

Layer and Groove creates premium vinyl record dividers designed for collectors who want clean, modern organization. Whether you alphabetize your LPs, sort by genre, or highlight favorites, our dividers keep your collection neat, accessible, and visually striking. Explore our full selection on Etsy.

Our Story

From custom dividers to storage essentials, we build simple, functional accessories to help you organize and display your records, CDs, DVDs, and other physical collections.

Launching our brand in 2026 focused on vinyl record dividers, we sold over 1,000 dividers in the first 6 months! We are thrilled to hear that our designs have been used (and loved) for music, film, books, and classroom organization . . . and as gifts!

We were pleasantly surprised that so many of you would have such an interest in our designs that we've slowly added a few other designs to our collection.

Thanks for dropping in, and if you have an idea/enhancement for our current products, or there's something we don't offer that you'd like to see send us a note!
